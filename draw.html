Character Design

Character design is a passion I have had for many years. Whenever I am creating a new story, I am often more drawn to the different people and relationships I can create than some of the other elements. This passion has led me to create many designs over the years, many I use in my free time for a variety of games I play with friends.

While my designs tend to be more complicated then ones that would be made for media like cartoons or video games, I still like to use basic elements like shapes and colors to create starting points for my designs. Which I then add onto, taking inspiration from a variety of fashion and genres.
